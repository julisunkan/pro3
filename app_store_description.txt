Cold Email Generator — AI-Powered Outreach Platform
=====================================================

SHORT DESCRIPTION (up to 255 characters):
Generate personalized AI cold emails in seconds. Upload leads, run campaigns, track analytics, and export to PDF, HTML, TXT, or DOCX — all powered by Groq's lightning-fast LLaMA AI.


FULL DESCRIPTION:
-----------------

Cold Email Generator is a professional AI-powered outreach platform that helps sales teams, freelancers, marketers, and entrepreneurs craft personalized cold emails at scale — without the guesswork.

Powered by Groq's blazing-fast LLaMA 3 model, every email is uniquely tailored to your product, audience, and tone. Simply describe your offer, pick a template, and let the AI do the heavy lifting.


KEY FEATURES:
-------------

AI Email Generation
Generate high-converting cold emails in seconds using four proven frameworks — Short Pitch, Problem-Solution, Quick Intro, and Partnership. Choose from four tones: Formal, Friendly, Persuasive, and Humorous.

Lead Management
Upload your leads via CSV (name, email, company, website) and manage your entire prospect list from one clean dashboard.

AI Personalization
Each lead receives a unique, personalized opening line based on their company website — scraped automatically using intelligent web parsing.

Campaign Engine
Group emails into named campaigns with custom products, tones, and templates. Run a full campaign to generate personalized emails for every lead in one click.

Campaign Analytics
Track opens, replies, open rate, and reply rate per campaign. Visualize performance with a built-in analytics dashboard.

Export Anywhere
Download any generated email as a formatted PDF, clean HTML, plain TXT, or Word DOCX file — all handled server-side for reliability.

Admin Panel
Customize the AI model, default sender name, tone, template, and your Groq API key — all from a built-in settings panel. No code required.

Progressive Web App
Install Cold Email Generator directly to your desktop or mobile device for a native app experience — works offline for cached pages.
